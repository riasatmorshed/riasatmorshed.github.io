Leadership

Details about my leadership and extra-curricular activities

Debating Club

General Secretary, BUET Debating Club(BUETDC)
Timeline: November, 2020- May, 2022
Before being elected as the General Secretary, I also served as "Publication Secretary", "Joint Secretary" respectively at the same club.
During my tenure at BUET Debating Club, I had undertaken some of the projects that showcase my leadership qualities.
Some of them are as follows:

Media-Scape Deep Dive: A Panel Discussion on Digital Media

During my time at BUET Debating Club, I always fostered a culture of critical thinking, study circle and discursive sessions. Not only that, I also tried to collaborate with other clubs from the campus. For instance, for this program, I took the initiave of involving our university film club BUET Film Society in this project. This project was a panel discussion consisting four of the most prominent and educational content creators and YouTubers in our country. We, in details, discussed the idea of digital media and how it is shaping our notion of media, as we know it. I, along with the then president of the club moderated the session. I lead organizing the session and was the main liaison person with another collaborator on behalf of my club. The video-footage of the program can be found here. Click here

BUET Debating Club Dialectics: A Study Circle on Contemporary Issues

One of the notable works I did while I was general secretary of BUET Debating Club, is leading a study-circle, called, "BUETDC Dialectics". The session used to be held periodically and it covered almost all the concurrent issues at hand. It did not limit itself to political or economical issues only, but also to sports, films, pop-culture as well. An important part of this project was documentation and I also managed the documentation team.
All the videos of a whole season can be found here Click here
